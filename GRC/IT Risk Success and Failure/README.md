# IT Risk: Success and Failure

**Type:** Written analysis paper (ITEC 380)

## Purpose

A comparative case-study essay examining why one company's high-stakes IT risk paid off while another's failed — and what separates the two beyond luck.

## Key topics

- **Netflix (success)** — migrating its entire operation to AWS was a major gamble that succeeded because it was executed gradually, with years of careful migration, constant testing, and adaptation, unlocking near-infinite scalability.
- **Target Canada (failure)** — a rushed launch of 100+ stores on an unproven inventory system that couldn't track actual stock, burning billions and forcing retreat from Canada in under two years.
- **The thesis** — preparation and organizational mindset, not luck, determine outcomes: successful risk-taking plans for failure and listens to early warning signs; failure comes from inflexible, rushed plans and a culture that ignores red flags.
